# Privacy Policy for Opportunity Cost Widget

**Effective Date:** 2024-02-05

## 1. Introduction
Opportunity Cost Widget ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

## 2. Data We Collect and How We Use It

### A. content_scripts & Amazon Data
The extension reads data from the Amazon product pages you visit (specifically price information) to calculate the "opportunity cost" of the item. 
- **Usage:** This data is used solely in real-time to display the calculation within the page or popup.
- **Storage:** This scanning happens momentarily in your browser's memory. We do not save your browsing history or product data to any external server.

### B. User Preferences (Storage Permission)
You may input data such as your hourly wage or investment return preferences.
- **Usage:** This data is used to personalize calculations.
- **Storage:** This data is stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage`. It never leaves your device and is not synchronized to us.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. The extension operates entirely client-side.

## 4. Updates to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Information on the most current version will be available on this page.
